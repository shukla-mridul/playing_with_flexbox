Abacot Ranger

The Abacot Ranger is a breed of domestic duck, initially known as the Hooded Ranger and as Streicherente

American Pekin

The Pekin or White Pekin is an American breed of domestic duck, raised primarily for meat.

Ancona

The Ancona is a breed of domestic duck, characterised by an unusual and variable broken-colored plumage pattern.

Call

The Call is a historic breed of small domestic duck.It is believed to have originated in the Netherlands

Aylesbury

The Aylesbury duck is a breed of domesticated duck, bred mainly for its meat and appearance.

Blekinge

The Blekinge duck (Swedish: Blekingeanka)is a medium-sized Swedish breed of domestic duck.

Cayuga

The Cayuga is an American breed of domestic duck.

Crested

The Crested is a breed of domestic duck. It was probably brought to Europe from the East Indies by Dutch ships.

Duclair

The Duclair duck is a type of Rouen duck named after the town of Duclair in Normandy

East Indie

The East Indie or Black East Indian is an ornamental breed of domestic duck.

German Pekin

The German Pekin, German: Deutsche Pekingente, is a European breed of domestic duck.

Hook Bill

The Hook Bill or Dutch Hookbill is a breed of domestic duck characterised by an unusual down-curved beak.
